Design Principles

Our design philosophy centers on clarity, professionalism, and data density.

ðŸŽ¯ Data Density

Maximize information visibility without overwhelming users. Reduce whitespace by ~50% compared to consumer apps while maintaining readability.

âœ¨ Subtle Effects

Use micro-animations and glassmorphism sparingly. Transitions should be fast (150-300ms) and purposeful.

ðŸŽ¨ Consistency

All components use design tokens. No hardcoded colors, spacing, or typography values.

Typography

We use Plus Jakarta Sans for all h1 headings to create a distinctive, modern look. Inter is used for all other text (body, paragraphs, buttons, etc.) as it's optimized for screens and provides excellent readability.
